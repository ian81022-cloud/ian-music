Ian Chang

Netflix Series Composer & Music Director

Ian has participated in various domestic and international music productions, including scoring for the Netflix series The Accidental Influencer, Encore Craft's musical Zeelandia, music and sound design for the Tainan Art Museum's exhibition Golden Years: Where Art Resounds, and composing the opening and closing theme music for the National Railway Museum. Currently, he works at composer Ming-Hsueh Lin's company as an arranger, copyist, and production assistant.

Specializing in blending electronic sound with orchestral music, Ian creates works with a strong sense of imagery and spatial depth. His style spans across diverse fields, including theater, film, exhibitions, and commercial brands. He continues to dedicate himself to teaching, scoring, and the practice of sound art.
